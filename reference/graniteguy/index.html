Frequently Asked Questions About Our Countertops

What types of countertops do you offer?

At Granite Guy Inc., we have a large selection of natural stones. This includes marble, granite, quartzite, soapstone, travertine, and limestone. We also offer almost every quartz brand and high-quality sintered stone, just like porcelain. Thanks to our network of local suppliers, we can quickly source just about anything you need.

How do I choose the right countertop material for my kitchen?

Each material has unique strengths—some are more durable, others low maintenance, and others offer distinct colors or patterns. The best way to decide is to see them in person and learn the differences firsthand. We'll guide you through everything so you can feel confident in your decision.

What is the process for installing countertops?

Our process begins with guiding you to choose the right material, then we finalize pricing and take precise measurements. After templating, we review all details and make any necessary adjustments before fabrication. Installation typically happens about one to two weeks later. We’ll keep communication clear and stay flexible especially for our local clients.

How do I maintain and care for my countertops?

To keep your countertops in great shape, clean them regularly with mild soap and water. Avoid harsh chemicals. Use cutting boards or trivets when needed. Each material has its own care needs, and we’re always here to offer expert advice tailored to yours.

Do you offer in‑home consultations for countertop selection?

Yes, we do! In-home consultations allow us to see your space.

We can answer your technical questions and help you choose the best materials. We consider lighting, layout, and style in our recommendations. We love working with local clients from the surrounding towns and being part of the community we serve.

What is the typical timeline for a countertop project from consultation to installation?

Installation typically occurs about one to two weeks after templating, once the team finalizes all details. Timelines can change based on the material, project size, and complexity. We always communicate clearly and stay flexible with scheduling. This is especially true for our local clients in MetroWest and Greater Boston.

Are your countertops suitable for both residential and commercial spaces?

Absolutely. We work with homeowners, builders, designers, and commercial clients. We enjoy helping people create beautiful spaces.

We work with builders and designers on custom homes and unique projects. In Boston, we focus on lobbies, common areas, and commercial desks. No matter the client, we tailor every project to bring their vision to life.

Do you provide a warranty on your countertops and installation services?

Yes—we back our work with a satisfaction guarantee and, when applicable, offer manufacturer warranties. We stand behind our craftsmanship while being transparent about the natural characteristics and limitations of each material.

Can I work with Granite Guy even if I don’t live nearby?

Absolutely. You don’t need to live nearby or visit us in person. We’ll guide you to a trusted slab supplier near you so you can choose your material without the long drive.

Once you’ve made your selection, the supplier ships it to us. We use advanced software called Slabsmith to create a digital layout.

This layout shows how your countertops will look, including pattern placement and flow. You can review and approve it from anywhere. It also helps us optimize the layout to reduce waste and deliver the best result.

How can I get a free quote for my countertop project?

If you know exactly what you want, you can quickly make a call, send an email, or fill out an online form to get started. If you are still exploring, that’s completely normal. With so many options available, we suggest visiting our showroom.

Seeing the materials in person helps you see the differences. This way, you can feel confident in your choice. We will take it from there.
